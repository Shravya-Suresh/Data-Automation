Notes & Conventions

Currency: All monetary values (sales, revenue, profit, assets, market_value, etc.) are in USD unless otherwise noted. The currency_yf field in the financial datasets indicates the original reporting currency from Yahoo Finance.

Suffixes: In BAV data, _PCT = percentage of respondents, _R = percentile rank (0–100), _C = continuous/composite score, _MS = mean score.

Duplicate formats: Several files exist in both CSV and XLSX (forbes_global2000, merged_companies). The CSV versions are typically the pipeline outputs; the XLSX versions are formatted copies.

Null values: Float columns with fewer non-null entries than total rows indicate companies that are not present in that source (e.g., Fortune fields are null for the ~1,576 companies that appear only in Forbes).

Primary key: universal_name is the canonical company identifier across all processed and master files. Use ticker to join with external financial data.
